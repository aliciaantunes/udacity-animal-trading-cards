Owl

With over 200 species living on every continent except Antarctica, owls have super-tuned senses that help them hunt prey all over the world. And they’re pretty darn cute, too..

  • Scientific Name:Strigiformes.
  • Average Lenght: 5–28 inches.
  • Habitat: coniferous forests, mountains, deserts, and plains.
  • Average Lifespan: 5 yo 12 years.

Owls can rotate their necks 270 degrees. A blood-pooling system collects blood to power their brains and eyes when neck movement cuts off circulation.
